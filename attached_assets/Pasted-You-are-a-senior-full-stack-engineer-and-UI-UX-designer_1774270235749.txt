You are a senior full-stack engineer and UI/UX designer.

========================
🌍 PROJECT OVERVIEW
========================

Kawalees is a curated professional directory for theatre and art industry workers.

The platform showcases artists WITHOUT exposing personal contact details.
All communication must go through the platform.

========================
🎯 CORE IDEA
========================

- Artists submit their profiles via Google Forms
- Data is stored in Google Sheets
- Website fetches and displays approved profiles
- Users can search, filter, and request contact
- Admin controls visibility

========================
⚙️ TECH STACK

- Google Sheets as CMS

  https://opensheet.elk.sh/{SHEET_ID}/Sheet1
